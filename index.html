THE AVENGER

we are peacekeepers

This is an American superhero film released in 2019, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures and is the fourth part of the Avengers film series. The film still has the presence of superhero characters such as Iron Man, Captain America, Hulk, Spider Man and some other characters. The film revolves around the Avengers realizing that their failure is unstoppable and then they take viewers to 5 years later with a time jump. That’s when Ant-Man reveals to Iron Man his ability to time travel and the time-traveling Avengers, stealing the Infinity Stones from the past in hopes of bringing back everything lost with a single reverse flick of hand. The Avengers team successfully obtained the 6 Infinity Stones and brought those who were wiped out by Thanos back to life. But soon after, the Avengers were also immediately attacked by Thanos traveling from 2014. They all fought together against Thanos, but he and his army were too strong. Iron Man jumps into the fray and uses nano-technology armor to transfer the Infinity Stones to his gauntlet. With 6 infinity stones in hand, Tony declares “I am Iron Man” before once again snapping his fingers. This time, half of the universe was no longer destroyed, but Thanos and his army were. However, the entire right part of the body was burned, leaving him with only a few minutes to stay with Pepper Potts, Spider-Man… and then die.
